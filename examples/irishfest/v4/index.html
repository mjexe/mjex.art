ABOUT
The Irish Fest of the Fox Cities is a not-for-profit event completely run by volunteers and funded by donations and sponsorships from charitable organizations and individuals.

The mission of the Irish Fest of the Fox Cities is to help improve the quality of life in the local communities by creating family-friendly events that promote cultural music, activities, and the education of the public on ethnic traditions and history. It is the vision of the festival and its organizers, through this mission, to help local nonprofits with fundraising and assist local businesses through increased tourism.

Volunteer
We are always very thankful for our amazing and dedicated volunteers and without them, the fest wouldn’t be what it is today. Irish Fest of the Fox Cities is an entirely not-for-profit event that relies on the generosity of other individuals and organizations to operate smoothly. If you would like to be a part of the great community that comes together to make the Irish Fest of the Fox Cities possible, please consider filling out the form found in the link below and we will find an opportunity that best fits your time commitment, abilities, and interests.

Sponsorships
What does it mean to be a sponsor of the Irish Fest of the Fox Cities? Being a sponsor means becoming a part of the Irish Fest family and encouraging the mission and goals of the festival and it’s organizers to provide a fun family-friendly experience and give back to the community. By sponsoring, you are directly contributing funds that are necessary to keep the event going every year and in-turn the festival can focus on what really matters, the experience of Irish Fest for the community.

Additionally, sponsoring Irish Fest means that we will proudly show off your logo as a supporter of our festival in places such as near the top of our website, on advertisements, and at the fest itself. Sponsors will also receive (number) free tickets to the event to share with their employees and family. All of this is just the very minimum of what we are willing to do to say our thanks to you for support the event.

Special arrangements such as sponsoring individual stages that the artists of the festival will perform on and much more. For more information please contact info@irishfestfoxcities.com

We appreciate our sponsors very much and want to extend our thanks again to anyone who has sponsored the event in the past or is even considering doing so in the future!
